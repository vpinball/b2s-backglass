Animations are a very interesting part of the 'B2S Backglass Designer'.


An animation in the sense of the designer is a sequence of illumination or illumination snippits switching on and off. An animation is made up of one or more animation steps and is maybe joined to a B2S or ROM id to start or stop the animation automatically.


Please open the backglass project file of 'Close Encounters of the Third Kind'.


'Close Encounters of the Third Kind' is a pretty cool pin, I love it. When I started to design the backglass I watched some videos at youtube. One of these videos had some cool animations at that flying saucer in the middle of the backglass. I didn't find any infos about this animation, I even don't know whether this animation is coming from the ROM or is added during some restoration of the pin.


Since I love these animation I added three very simple and discreet animations at the saucer. Let's have a look at these added animations.
