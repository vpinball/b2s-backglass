Click the rightmost button of these buttons, 'Show illumination with accurate intensity' or click the menu item 'Illumination' -> 'Show illumination with accurate intensity'


The result will be this very light illumination over "SHOW"


Take a first look at the "Illumination tool window". Right now the only interesting thing is the 'Intensity' slider. Change the intensity from level '1' to level '2'.


The illumination will  be changed immediately. This is the result.
