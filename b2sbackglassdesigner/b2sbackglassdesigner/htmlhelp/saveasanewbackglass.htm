Save as a new backglass

Creating a first B2S backglass ›› Reusing an existing B2S backglass project ››

Please click 'File' -> 'Save copy as ...'.


The 'Settings' dialog is opened automatically since we have to enter some new settings data.


Let's enter a 'Project name' and a matching 'Backglass (file) name'. Click 'Ok'.


Now it's pretty important to import  a new background image since the designer wants to move and resize all score and illumination frames matching the new background image resolution.


So please click on 'Yes' (or 'Ja' or 'Si' or 'Oui' or ...) and import the new background image.


Save your new backglass project and that's it. Please show the score and/or illumination frames and you can see all frames moved and resized according the new resolution. Now it's your turn to move everything to a perfect location.
