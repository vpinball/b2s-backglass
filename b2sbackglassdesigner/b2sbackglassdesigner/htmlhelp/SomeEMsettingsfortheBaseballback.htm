Some EM settings for the 'Baseball' backglass

Please open the 'Baseball' backglass project since I don't want to show you the whole design process for 'Baseball'.


It's an EM table from 1970 so it's a pretty simple backglass. There is no flashing and no animation. Just a bit illumination and some reels for the scores, the homeruns and the credits.


Please click on 'Reels & LEDs' -> 'Choose Reel or LED type ...'.


Let's look at some settings for the EM score reels.


Two reels are already selected, 'EM reel 2' and 'EM credit reel 2'. Very interesting are the three text boxes below the EM reel list view. Choose the

  • Rolling direction: 'Up' or 'Down'
  • Rolling interval (in milliseconds): This is the time that a reel needs to switch completely from one number to the next.
  • Intermediate image count: The count of automatically rendered images for a soft changeover from one number to the next.
