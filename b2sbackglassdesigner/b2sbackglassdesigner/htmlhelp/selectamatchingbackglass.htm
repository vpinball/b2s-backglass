Our goal is a backglass for 'Lost World'. The pin is from 1978, a 'Bally' machine. Of all my backglass projects the nearest is 'Playboy', a 'Bally' machine too, from 1978, too. Perfect.


Open 'Playboy' in the designer. Hi Heff.


What we see is:

  • A  classical 4 player pin with 7-segmented LEDs
  • A few backglass lights without connection to a ROM ID. Most of them, not all of them are set to 'Always on'.
  • A lot backglass lights with ROM IDs. About 10 joined to 'Lamp 13', looks like a 'general illumination', and a lot bulbs joined to ROM IDs for some writings, for player 1 to 4 illumination, and so on.
  • Background image has a resolution of 1469 x 1354 pixels.


Time to start the new project with saving as a new backglass.
