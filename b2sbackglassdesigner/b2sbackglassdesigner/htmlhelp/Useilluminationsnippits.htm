Use illumination snippits

Creating a first B2S backglass ›› More interesting designer stuff ››

'Illumination snippits' are small illuminated parts of an illumination image.


Start with your standard background image. Design some lighting in your paint app, cut these small parts of your background image, note the location of these snippits and import them into the designer at ...

  • 'Illumination' -> 'Add a new illumination snippit'


The big advantage is: You can add designer created illumination when you want to or you need them. Your backglass can be a mix of illumination snippits and frames.


A good example where to use this technique is the 'match arrow' of the 'Bally' pins from the late 1970's and 1980's. This is what I mean ...

'Match' is a designer create illumination, the arrow is an imported illumination snippit.
