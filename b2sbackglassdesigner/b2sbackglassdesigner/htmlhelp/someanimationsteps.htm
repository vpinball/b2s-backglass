Okay, here is our animation from 'Close Encounters of the Third Kind'.


Each step is a combination of lamps going on, waiting for some time, lamps going off and waiting once again. The declaration strings for lamps, "Lamps on" or "Lamps off", are comma separated lists of lamp names. The waiting infos, "Wait intervals after On" or "Wait intervals after Off", are a multiplier of the interval of the animation header.


  • 'Lamps On (",")'
    A comma separated list of illumination names. These illumination bulbs are switched on with this step.
  • 'Wait intervals after On'
    Waiting time multiplier after switching the lamps on. This number is multiplied with the animation header interval in milliseconds.
  • 'Lamps Off (",")'
    A comma separated list of illumination names. These illumination bulbs are switched off with this step.
  • 'Wait intervals after Off'
    Waiting time multiplier after switching the lamps off. This number is multiplied with the animation header interval in milliseconds.


Configure the steps with the four buttons to the right of the steps list. The first button is adding a new step at the end of the list, the second is removing the currently selected step, button 3 and 4 are moving the currently selected step upstairs or downstairs.

To edit a step just click on the step entry in the list and edit in the four text boxes below.


Check out the animation steps of our sample project. Eight steps are already added. Please have a look at step 1 to 3 above.

  • Step 1 is switching the lamp named "ST1" on, is waiting for 2 x 200 milliseconds, is switching no lamp off and isn't waiting any longer.
  • Step 2 is switching the lamps "ST2" and "ST3" on, is waiting for 4 x 200 milliseconds, is switching lamp "ST2" off and is waiting for 200 milliseconds.
  • Step 3 is switching the lamp "ST4" on, is waiting for 2 x 200 milliseconds, is switching lamp "ST1" off and is waiting for 200 milliseconds.


And so on.
