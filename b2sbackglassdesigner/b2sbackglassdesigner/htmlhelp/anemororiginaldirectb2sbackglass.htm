From now on a requirement is that you have some simple knowledge about 'Visual Pinball' table scripting. I will explain a lot but I cannot explain everything.


As I mentioned earlier the big difference between an EM or original table on the one side and SS tables on the other side is the use of 'Visual PinMAME' as a connection to the solid state table code. For an EM or original table there isn't such a code.
